# Roadmap

## 0.1 Foundation
Standalone web app, PostgreSQL schema, dashboard, pantry, recipes, shopping scaffold and Health Connect permission app.

## 0.2 Pantry and receipts
Pantry transactions, expiry and use-soon are live. Manual receipt entry, duplicate protection, line review, food classification and Pantry import are implemented. Pantry and Shopping support a shared saved-product picker and continuous live barcode scanning with reusable product recognition. Receipt-image OCR remains a future enhancement.

## 0.3 Shopping optimiser
Persisted shopping lists, item grouping, mobile check-off, completed-item cleanup, low-stock Pantry quick-adds and receipt-derived price history are implemented. Manual Woolworths, Coles and ALDI price capture, individual-product comparison, whole-list retailer estimates and split-shop savings are implemented. Live retailer connectors, Springwood list ordering and aisle data remain future work.

## 0.4 Health
Health Connect aggregation and secure sync for hydration, steps, calories, exercise, sleep and weight.
